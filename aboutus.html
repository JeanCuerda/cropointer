About Us

Project crOpOint is a community platform revolutionizing urban farming through sharing and empowering each colorful plant experiences through the spirit of bayanihan.

The platform will be optimizing each urban farmers’/gardeners’ journey in their urban farming/gardening by providing them a unified digital space where they can showcase, foster and share their own stories through images easily.

By just simply sharing your own images in the platform, you can help a simple but struggling farmers across the nation. This will also serve as your digital hoe in plowing the vast earth and sowing act of good seeds and deeds.
